Our story

We are a team of highly skilled leaders within the BPO Sector from Cape Town, South Africa. Winner of the CCMG Global Outsourcing destination of choice 2021. As a collective, we have worked across the largest BPO partnerships over the past 10+ years. Delivering Business transformation, Change Management, L&D, QA, CX Operations, Resource planning Enterprise solution SME's, Partnership Management, Business continuity, Customer insights both upstream and downstream.

weproliferate was born in 2020 and over the last 2 years, developed slowly but very strategically to what is being presented today, the more we understood Leading tech business processes, the more we saw the knowledge gap arise for all other industries in the future. Covid 19 aggravated this even more and caused a delay in our plans as we were key to the work from home need to succeed across the various outsources we were employed with.

We knew the following:
We are primed for an influx of tech adoption according to our analysis and research, the legacy educational system was behind the curve and needed support, we have been trained by the L&D framework that we later became experts on, we then committed to provide as much as would allow free tech education which is not merely your basic computer literacy type course. Extensive research done on the 2030 goals of sustainability shaped our 3 cores...Sustainability, Ecosystem based and Rapid Growth whicht is only the start of weproliferate.

Being fully focused on technology adoption, servicing our very own solutions was an absolute must. Being immersed in global enterprise customer queries, which we feel is more powerful than most realize, were unpacked, understood and translated into a change request or CX transformational pilot, these types of initiatives gave us an understanding of integration and customer impact that very few have, we of course allowed these experiences to lead our methodologies for a model that is always in a state of adaptive growth and improvement
